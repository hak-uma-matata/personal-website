ABOUT
---------
It is dark and if you cannot find the light switch in time, you are trapped in this cave for the rest of eternity until you turn into a burgeoning mass of bones. When the game starts, you have a limited amount of time to note the randomized position of the light switch before it disappears. Then you need to get to the light switch before the timer runs out. Your score is based on how little time you take to get to the switch position- the less time, the higher the score.

---------
CONTROLS
---------
Enter the game with START / ENTER
Move the player (your square) with the ARROW KEYS
If at any point you wish to exit the game, press SELECT / BACKSPACE
